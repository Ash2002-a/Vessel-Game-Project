Blood Vessel Cutting Game

A serious game to study the effect of limited field of view in keyhole surgery

Welcome to the blood vessel game. The game challenges you to complete a simple cutting task as many times as possible in 30 seconds. We gather anonymous data to test whether how much you can see affects your performance. By participating in the game you will contribute to making keyhole surgery safer and faster, and hopefully you will also have some fun.
