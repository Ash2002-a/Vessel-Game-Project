How to Play

  • Controls:
    • Mouse: Click and drag across vessels to cut them
  • Scoring:
    • White endpoints: Correct vessel (+10 points)
    • White-Black endpoints: Wrong vessel (-50 points)
  • Active Distractions (Click to handle):

    Blood Leak

    Click to stop the leak

    Warning Alert

    Click to acknowledge

    Instrument Request

    Click to respond

  • Background Distractions (No action needed):
    • 📞 Incoming Calls: Just ignore
    • ❤️ Heart Rate Alerts: Just ignore
    • 🔊 Voice Commands: Just listen
  • Level Progression:
    • Level 1-2: Basic vessel cutting
    • Level 3: Limited field of view
    • Level 4-5: Intertwined vessels with distractions
    • Level 6-7: Multiple complex vessels with background distractions
  • Time Limit: 30 seconds per level
  • New vessels appear automatically
  • NEW: Audio cues for gameplay events

Welcome to the Vessel Game!

This is the surgical environment where you'll be performing vessel operations. The background provides a realistic surgical setting for your tasks.

Your Mission:

  • Identify and cut the correct vessels
  • Handle various surgical distractions
  • Maintain focus while managing multiple tasks
  • Complete each level within the time limit

Game Elements

Vessels

Cut the correct vessels (white endpoints) and avoid incorrect ones (black endpoints)

Field of View

In later levels, you'll have a limited field of view that follows your cursor

Distractions

Handle various surgical distractions like blood leaks, warning alerts, and instrument requests by clicking on them

Background Events

📞

Incoming calls, heart rate alerts, and voice commands are there to distract you. No action is required.

How to Play

Controls

  • Click and drag to cut vessels
  • Click on distractions to handle them
  • Pay attention to background events
  • Complete each level within 30 seconds

Scoring

  • +10 points for correct vessel cuts
  • -50 points for incorrect vessel cuts
  • Handle distractions to maintain focus

Level Progression

  • Level 1: Basic vessel cutting
  • Level 2: Limited field of view
  • Level 3: Introduction of distractions
  • Level 4: Intertwined vessels
  • Level 5-7: All elements combined

Tips

  • Focus on correct vessels first
  • Handle distractions quickly
  • Watch your field of view
  • Keep track of the timer
